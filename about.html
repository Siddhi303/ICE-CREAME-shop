Our Story


At Scoopies, our love affair with ice cream began generations ago. It all started in a cozy kitchen, where our founder, Grandma Lila, churned out heavenly frozen treats for her family. The aroma of vanilla, the swirls of chocolate, and the laughter around the table—it was pure magic. Fast forward to today, and we've transformed Grandma Lila's cherished recipes into a delightful ice cream haven. Our mission? To spread happiness, one scoop at a time.

Community and Connection


Scoopiesisn't just a place to satisfy your sweet tooth; it's a community. We've seen first dates blossom over double scoops, families celebrate birthdays with towering sundaes, and friends share secrets over shared cones. Join us for our weekly Sundae Socials, where laughter echoes off the walls, and sprinkles rain down like confetti. We're more than an ice cream parlor—we're a memory-maker
